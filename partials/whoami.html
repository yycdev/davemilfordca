Who I Am

A Canadian software developer by trade.
Born and (mostly) raised in Calgary, Alberta, and currently residing there.
As a child, I also lived for four years in Northern California.
After university, I lived for five years in Toronto, Ontario.


I attended the University of Calgary, where I received a B.Sc. in Computer Science (with concentration in Software Engineering).

Geekery


I've always been a geek.

I built legos, watched Star Trek, owned all the game consoles, built computers, and even programmed as a kid. Always had that fascination with how things worked. I wanted to learn how to do it all.


I'm a builder: I always want to make something. It's not enough to play games, I need to make them too. It's also how I learn -- I'm a hands-on learner and I want to learn by making. That's where this website came from - I wanted to learn a bunch of new technologies. And I didn't want to read about it. I wanted to learn by building.


But sometimes I just want to relax, too. I'm a video gamer (still own all the consoles - plus the PC). My Steam game collection has 191 games in it, though I've probably only played 10% of them (Steam sales...). I love strategy (Civilization) and Grand Strategy (Paradox games), but sometimes I just want to unwind with some more mindless games - Diablo III, Halo, Team Fortress 2, etc.

Music

Music is a big part of my life. Discovering new stuff, rediscovering the old stuff. I'll spend a good couple hours per day with music on, whether it's through my phone, in my car, at work, or on the couch with a vinyl playing. (Yes, I have a small but growing vinyl collection -- you can blame my brother for introducing me to them).


As a kid, I took piano lessons for a number of years. I'm also a self-taught guitar player, though I'm not any good. I play acoustic -- feels more proper unless I've actually got a band to play in. There aren't enough hours in the day to dedicate time to becoming good at it, but it's something that's on my bucket list.


I can get into most types of music, but I gravitate towards Alernative Rock and Indie Rock.

Life

I live in Calgary, Alberta, Canada -- in the foothills of the rockies. I'm not an urbanite, though. I own a house in the quiet suburbs with my partner of over eleven years and our two cats.


When I'm not at work or at home, I like to make use of the mountains. I haven't done it as much as I'd like to lately, but that will change. I love hiking and snowshoeing: the serenity and clean air of the mountains, and the quietness of solitude or the accompaniment of good friends. It's also a great excuse to take some photos! I'm an amateur camper as well: I really enjoy being able to disconnect with the digital world and reconnect with the other world.


My brother got me into Scotch many years ago. It took some experience to get into it as he did, but I'm glad I did. I've been to numerous whiskey shows and have tried dozens of different Scotch and other whiskeys. I mostly have single-malts at home (Glenfarclas 17 & Highland Park 12 are my favourite go-to drinks), but I've got nothing against the blended! People may think I'm an alcoholic because I've got over a dozen bottles of single malts on my shelf at any given time. I'm not, I promise! They last years! I love variety.
