Who I Am

A Canadian software developer by trade.
Born and (mostly) raised in Calgary, Alberta, and currently residing there.
As a child, I also lived for four years in Northern California.
After university, I lived for five years in Toronto, Ontario.


I attended the University of Calgary, where I received a B.Sc. in Computer Science (with concentration in Software Engineering).

Geekery

I've always been a geek.


I built legos, watched Star Trek, owned all the game consoles, built computers, and even programmed as a kid. Always had that fascination with how things worked. I wanted to learn how to do it all.


I'm a builder: I always want to make something. It's not enough to play games, I need to make them too. It's also how I learn -- I'm a hands-on learner and I want to learn by making. That's where this website came from - I wanted to learn a bunch of new technologies. And I didn't want to read about it. I wanted to learn by building.

Photography

I took a photography course in high school, borrowing my dad's 1980s-era Canon SLR, and I loved it. When I graduated from university, I bought a Canon digital SLR and have been shooting with it since.
